### 2. Information We Collect

The categories of personal information we collect depend on how you interact with us, our Services, and the requirements of applicable law. We collect information you provide to us, information we obtain automatically when you use our Services, and information from other sources.

#### Information You Provide Directly (Registered Users Only)

We may collect the following personal information that you provide to us:

- **Account Creation**: When you create an account, we may collect information such as username, email address, or password.
- **Communications with Us**: When you request information about our Services, subscribe to our newsletter, request customer or technical support, or otherwise communicate with us, we may collect personal information such as email address.
- **Surveys**: We may contact you to participate in surveys. If you decide to participate, you may be asked to provide certain information, which may include personal information.
- **Business Development**: We may collect personal information from individuals and third parties to evaluate and pursue potential business opportunities.

#### Automatically Collected Information (Registered Users Only)

When you use our Services, we may automatically collect personal information:

- **Website Usage Data**: We may automatically collect certain information such as your Internet Protocol (IP) address, user settings, MAC address, cookie identifiers, mobile carrier, mobile advertising identifiers, browser or device information, location information (including approximate location inferred from IP address), and Internet service provider. We may also automatically collect information about your use of our Services, such as pages you access, links you click, content types you interact with, frequency and duration of your activities, and other information about how you use our Services.

- **Service Usage Data**: We may collect other types of technical information about your use of our Services, such as telemetry metrics. This technical information is not associated with your personal information or Customer Data and is used solely to improve our Services.

- **Cookies and Tracking Technologies**: We and third parties that provide content or other functionality on our Services may use cookies, pixel tags, local storage, and other technologies to automatically collect information:
  - **Cookies**: Small text files placed in device browsers to store preferences and facilitate your experience.
  - **Pixel Tags/Web Beacons**: Code embedded in our Services to collect engagement information and track email interactions.
  
- **Our use of these technologies falls into the following categories**:
  - **Strictly Necessary**: Technologies required for Service access, security, and fraud prevention.
  - **Marketing and Analytics**: Technologies to evaluate Service performance and understand usage patterns.
  - **Advertising**: Technologies to provide enhanced functionality and track preferences.

- **Analytics**: We may use technologies and third-party tools to process analytics information on our Services.

- **Social Media Platforms**: Our Services may contain social media buttons and interactive widgets. Your interactions with these platforms are governed by their respective privacy policies.

Payment and Billing Information: If you purchase a subscription or service from us, we may collect payment and billing information.
